# Privacy Policy for Flatmates

**Last Updated**: November 17, 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to Flatmates ("we", "our", or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Flatmates mobile application ("the App").

By using Flatmates, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide

**Account Information**
- Google Account information (email address, name, profile picture)
- Information provided during account creation

**Household Data**
- Household names and member information
- Expense details (amounts, descriptions, categories, dates)
- Shopping list items and details
- Task and todo information
- Any notes or comments you add

### 2. Automatically Collected Information

**Device Information**
- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- App version
- Unique device identifiers

**Usage Data**
- Features you use
- Time spent in the app
- Error logs and crash reports
- Push notification preferences

**Location Data**
- We do NOT collect your precise location data
- We may collect approximate location based on IP address for service delivery

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

### Primary Purposes
1. **Service Delivery**: Providing core app functionality (expense tracking, shopping lists, tasks)
2. **Account Management**: Creating and managing your user account
3. **Communication**: Sending notifications about household activities
4. **Collaboration**: Enabling shared features with your flatmates

### Secondary Purposes
1. **Improvement**: Analyzing usage patterns to improve the app
2. **Support**: Responding to your support requests
3. **Security**: Detecting and preventing fraud or abuse
4. **AI Features**: Processing receipts and providing smart suggestions (data not stored long-term)

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage
- Your data is stored securely on our servers using industry-standard encryption
- We use PostgreSQL database with encrypted connections
- Data centers are located in [Your Region]
- Local data is cached on your device using secure storage (AsyncStorage)

### Security Measures
- All data transmissions use HTTPS/TLS encryption
- Passwords are not stored (we use Google OAuth)
- JWT tokens for secure authentication
- Regular security audits and updates
- Access controls and monitoring

### Data Retention
- Active user data: Retained while your account is active
- Deleted accounts: Data permanently deleted within 30 days
- Backup retention: 90 days
- Anonymized analytics: May be retained indefinitely

## Data Sharing and Third Parties

### We DO Share Data With:

**Household Members**
- Expenses, shopping lists, and tasks are visible to all household members
- Profile information (name, email, picture) is visible to household members

**Service Providers**
- Google Cloud Platform (hosting)
- Google Sign-In (authentication)
- AI Providers (OpenAI/Google Gemini for AI features - data not stored)

### We DO NOT:
- Sell your personal data to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use your data for purposes other than stated in this policy
- Access your Google account beyond the requested permissions

## Your Rights and Choices

### You Have the Right To:

1. **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
2. **Correction**: Update or correct your information
3. **Deletion**: Request deletion of your account and data
4. **Portability**: Export your data in a machine-readable format
5. **Opt-Out**: Disable push notifications or AI features

### How to Exercise Your Rights:
- **Account Settings**: Manage preferences in the app
- **Data Export**: Contact us at privacy@flatmates.app
- **Account Deletion**: Use the "Delete Account" option in Profile settings
- **Support**: Email support@flatmates.app

## Children's Privacy

Flatmates is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

## AI Features and Data Processing

### Receipt OCR
- Receipt images are processed by AI providers (OpenAI/Google Gemini)
- Images are not permanently stored by us or AI providers
- Processed data (amounts, merchants) is stored in your account
- You can disable this feature in settings

### Smart Categorization
- Expense descriptions are analyzed to suggest categories
- Processing happens in real-time, data is not retained by AI providers
- You can always override AI suggestions

### Task Suggestions
- Based on common household tasks, not your personal data
- Suggestions can be ignored or disabled

## Cookies and Tracking

- We do NOT use cookies (this is a mobile app, not a website)
- We do NOT use third-party tracking or analytics services
- Local storage is used only for app functionality (login state, preferences)
- Push notification tokens are stored securely
